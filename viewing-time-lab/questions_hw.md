## Goal Alignment 
Align with stakeholders on what success means before interpreting any numbers.

1. Is the company looking  for  ideal word/slide count?
2. In what timeframe was this was the data collected? 
3. What KPI's are important for you?
4. How do you  intend to use this data moving forward?
## Define Over-performance 
Consider what it would mean if the treatment dramatically exceeded expectations.
1. If viewing time exceeded  the target by 2x or more, what thresold would trigger a full rollout vs. further testing?
2. Could a dramatic spike in viewing time indicate a negative experience - like users leaving content paused or confused?
3. What would over-performance in one user segment but not others tell us about the treatment's true effeciveness?
